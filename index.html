A series of events with the goal of bringing together and developing a community of artists and musicians in the Öresund region who work with audiovisual creative technology.

Helping people to build their skills, meet collaborators, and reach audiences interested in what they do. The goal is to uplift the whole community, and bring new people into it over time.
